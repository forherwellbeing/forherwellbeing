FHW Supplements will be the first line built entirely from women's metabolic science — condition-specific, evidence-graded, and designed to work alongside your personalised program, not replace it.

PCOS Formula
Myo-inositol + D-chiro-inositol (40:1 ratio), NAC, zinc, Vitamin D3+K2, spearmint extract — backed by 50+ clinical trials.
Metabolic & Weight Support
Berberine, magnesium glycinate, alpha-lipoic acid, chromium picolinate, probiotic blend — targeting insulin resistance at its roots.

The Science Frontier

Finding Answers No One
Has Found Yet

For Her Wellbeing is a deep science company. We are not satisfied with existing knowledge — we are actively investigating, researching, and building pathways to therapies that do not yet exist for women.

Microbiome Personalisation

Women's gut microbiome composition differs profoundly by hormonal phase, life stage, and condition. We are building condition-specific microbiome profiles for PCOS, autoimmune, obesity, and menopause — enabling personalised probiotic and prebiotic protocols with clinical precision.

Research phase · 2026–2027
Peptide Therapies

Peptides — short amino acid chains — are emerging as powerful tools for hormonal optimisation, weight management (GLP-1 analogs beyond Ozempic), and immune modulation. We are researching women-specific peptide protocols that address the hormonal root causes conventional medicine treats only with symptomatic drugs.

Research collaboration · 2027
Cell Therapy for Autoimmune

Regulatory T-cell (Treg) therapy, CAR-T cell approaches, and stem cell-based interventions are showing early promise in autoimmune conditions that disproportionately affect women. We are building collaborations with research institutions to create clinical pathways for women who have exhausted conventional options.

Collaboration roadmap · 2027–2028
Gene Expression Intelligence

Nutrigenomics — how your genes determine your response to specific foods, supplements, and lifestyle factors — is transforming personalised medicine. We are building gene-based health intelligence panels specifically for women's conditions: PCOS predisposition, autoimmune risk, estrogen metabolism pathways, and metabolic phenotyping.

Development phase · 2027
