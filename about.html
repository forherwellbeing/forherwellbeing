She holds a PhD in Metabolic Science from Heidelberg University, Germany — one of Europe's leading research universities — plus an MSc in Biotechnology and formal yoga therapy certifications. Her work is not about prescribing drugs or replacing your gynaecologist. It is about understanding why your body is behaving the way it is, and using evidence-based nutrition, yoga, and lifestyle science to correct it at the root. Dr. Raga does not diagnose or prescribe — she investigates and corrects root causes through metabolic science, nutrition and yoga therapy.
