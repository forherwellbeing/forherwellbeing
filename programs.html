Questions

Program FAQs

Are the workshops online or in-person? +
Both! Yoga sessions are available online via video call and in-person (Hyderabad). Diet consultations and community sessions are primarily online so women across India and internationally can join.
What is the batch size for each workshop? +
Batches are kept intentionally small (8–15 women) to ensure Dr. Raga Deepthi can give personal attention to each participant. This is not a mass programme — it's a genuine community.
Do I need prior yoga experience? +
No — all workshops welcome beginners. The yoga is adapted to each woman's current fitness level, health condition, and goals. You do not need any prior experience.
What does the consultation fee cover? +
The ₹500 consultation fee covers your initial 1:1 session with Dr. Raga Deepthi where she reviews your health history, goals, and recommends the most appropriate program. Workshop fees are separate and discussed after the initial consultation.
Can I join if I'm pregnant or postpartum? +
Yes — the Obesity Workshop has a dedicated pregnancy and postpartum track. Dr. Raga Deepthi has specific expertise in gestational weight management, postpartum recovery, and postnatal yoga.
